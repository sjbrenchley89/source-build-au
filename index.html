What we supply

Ten product
categories.

We supply across the full building product spectrum — from structure and envelope through to finished interiors. All products sourced and supplied with relevant Australian compliance documentation as standard.

Cabinetry & Joinery
E0 board · CARB P2 · Soft-close
Kitchens, bathroom vanities, laundry joinery, wardrobes, and custom cabinetry to your layout. Flat-pack or pre-assembled, E0-compliant.
Windows, Glazing & Aluminium Systems
AS 2047 · NCC · WERS rated
Fixed, sliding, awning, casement, and bifold windows. Aluminium framing systems and glazing. Custom sizes to your drawings.
Doors, Hardware & Access
AS 1905 · NCC compliant
Residential and commercial doors, fire-rated assemblies, hardware suites, and door frames. Internal and external applications.
Roofing Systems & Façade Cladding
NCC compliant · BCA Sec J
Metal roofing, insulated panels, facade cladding systems, and rainscreen systems for residential and commercial applications.
Stone Benchtops, Countertops & Vanities
Engineered · Natural stone
Engineered stone and natural stone benchtops, kitchen counters, and bathroom vanity tops. Custom sizes and edge profiles to specification.
Plumbing Fixtures & Sanitaryware
WaterMark · WELS · AS 3718
WaterMark-certified tapware, mixers, basins, pans, cisterns, and valves. WELS ratings supplied with every product, every time.
Bathrooms, Kitchens & Wet Areas
WaterMark · NCC · E0 board
Complete bathroom and kitchen supply packages — cabinetry, sanitaryware, tapware, stone, and accessories. Coordinated from a single source.
Engineered & Floating Timber Flooring
E0/E1 · AS/NZS 1080 · NAUF
Engineered hardwood and floating timber flooring systems. Multiple species, finishes, and board widths. E0-compliant substrates throughout.
Tiles & Surface Finishes
ISO 13006 · AS 4459 · Slip rated
Porcelain, ceramic, stone-look, and large-format tiles. Feature walls, floor finishes, and facade cladding. Project volumes, batch-matched.
